Description:
  Jupyter-tldraw connects the React whiteboard tldraw to the Jupyter notebook ecosystem.
  - GitHub: 271 stars, 22 forks, actively maintained since March 2023.
  - Downloads: ~58,800 all-time PyPI downloads, ~1,600 in the last 30 days.
  - Demo on Twitter with 340k views: https://x.com/kolibril13/status/1736693741178491204
  - Ecosystem relevance: the simplest way to bring drawings on a 2D canvas into a programmatic data-science workflow.
